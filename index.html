Our Story

...it was a sunny day, colored rays penetrated through a yellow window blind into a sitting room. Lawrence was on his way on the wall of Instagram when he stumbled upon the picture of a beautiful lady. His heart was full of love for her immediately and he thought in his heart, "Lord, would you give me this mountain". So, he prayed and he heard "yes". He was happy. He went to meet this beautiful lady in Kebbi State where she was serving as a Corp member and he tendered his humble proposal.
"Beautiful, I am in deep love for you" he said "I really want to marry you as my wife in the nearest future".
"Okay." The beautiful lady replied with a sheepish smile on her face.
Before then, Lawrence was afraid, he thought the beautiful lady would not consider him or care about his proposal. But the beautiful lady turned out to be a good girl... what Lawrence desired most!
"So, are you telling me you will pray about it?" Lawrence questioned the beautiful lady in amusement.
"Yes, I would."
On hearing this, Lawrence was about to start singing one of his songs, "I chant them open... the gates of your heart", but he remembered he was still sitting right in front of this beautiful lady.
FOUR MONTHS LATER...
The beautiful lady sent a text message to Lawrence... "YES".
... Lawrence was sitting in his living room when the message entered his phone.
"Wow. Could this true?" He said loudly. He grabbed his car key and headed for the parking lot and drove out to the beautiful lady's place. On getting there, he met her at the gate. Without saying good morning, he muttered to her, "is that the best way to say YES?" He grinned widely after saying this.
"When the heart is full of love, the best way to relay the message of the heart is in a simple language." The beautiful lady replied. Immediately, Lawrence hugged and whispered into her ears, "I'm gonna spend the rest of my life with you forever!"
The beautiful lady is Darasimi!
